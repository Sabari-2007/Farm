Bihar Saur Kranti Sinchai Yojana
Solar energy irrigation with 75% subsidy.

Farmers receive solar pumps for irrigation. 75% subsidy is provided by the government, and farmers contribute the remaining 25%.

Bihar Rajya Fasal Sahayata Yojana
Financial assistance during crop loss due to calamities.

Farmers receive financial incentives in case of crop loss due to natural calamities. During application, they must select the crops, mark sowing areas, and provide self-attested documents for verification.

Bihar Diesel Grant Scheme
Subsidy on diesel for irrigation.

The Bihar Government provides subsidies on diesel for farmers, reducing irrigation costs and supporting smallholder farmers.
